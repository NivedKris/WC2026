Shipping Policy — WC 2026

WC 2026 does not sell or ship any physical goods. All payments made on our platform are digital contributions toward community events and fan-zone activities for the upcoming World Cup.

As such:

  • There is no shipping or delivery involved.
  • Supporters may receive digital acknowledgements or participation updates via email.

Any physical events or merchandise (if introduced in the future) will have their own separate communication and timelines.

Thank you for being part of our mission to make the World Cup celebration bigger and better in our communities!
