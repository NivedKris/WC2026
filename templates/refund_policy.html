3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund & Cancellation Policy — WC 2026

At WC 2026, every contribution helps us create community-based World Cup experiences — such as local fan zones, screening events, and decorations.

Since funds are used to plan and organize group activities, payments are generally non-refundable. However, we do consider refund requests under certain conditions:

  • Duplicate payments made accidentally
  • Incorrect payment amount due to technical errors

If you wish to request a refund, please email us at mark.organisation@gmail.com within 3 days of your transaction. Our team will review your request and respond within 5–7 business days.

Please note that contributions are voluntary and not linked to any betting, prize, or profit. Funds are solely used for fan engagement and event expenses.
